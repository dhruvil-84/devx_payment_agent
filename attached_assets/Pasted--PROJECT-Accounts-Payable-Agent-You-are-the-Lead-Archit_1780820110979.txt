---------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS PROBLEM
---------------------------------------------------

Finance teams process hundreds of invoices.

Knowledge about vendors, disputes, approval workflows, and recurring exceptions exists only in employees' heads.

We need an AI system that:

- remembers vendor behavior
- remembers historical exceptions
- remembers approval patterns
- remembers dispute history
- learns recurring patterns
- uses memory to make smarter decisions

The AI should behave like a senior finance employee who never forgets.

Core Demonstration:

Vendor GlobalTech has a history of tax mismatches and invoice disputes.

When a new invoice arrives from GlobalTech, the system must automatically recognize historical patterns and recommend escalation.

This memory-driven decision is the main demo.
